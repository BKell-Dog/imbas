### Artists, Engineers, Builders

{{ site.title }} is an electronics consulting and contracting firm that has often been a hardware partner for technical founders. We are a small team of tech experts who *left the corporate world* and now help businesses enhance their existing systems or build new ones from the ground up. We take on a small number of jobs at a time and give our *full efforts* to each one.

The word *Imbas* is of [Old Irish](https://lrc.la.utexas.edu/eieol/iriol/0) origin and means roughly *enlightenment* or *inspiration*. The name reflects our beliefs that the best solutions to seemingly-impossible problems are those which enhance scientific principles using some element of artistic or creative inspiration.

Our thesis is that *beautiful engineering* originates in the same part of the mind as beautiful art. In all of our work we seek to achieve the *merger of art and engineering*, to create something elegant yet functional, intuitive yet inspired, time-tested yet new.
