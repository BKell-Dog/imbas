### Artists, Engineers, Builders

Imbas Hard Tech is an electronics outsourcing and consulting firm that goes deep on complicated problems to deliver elegant solutions. We are a small team of tech experts who left the corporate world and now help businesses enhance their existing systems or build new ones from the ground up.

The word *Imbas* is of Old Irish origin and means roughly *enlightenment* or *inspiration*. The name reflects our beliefs that the best solutions to seemingly-impossible problems are those which build upon scientific principles using some element of artistic or creative inspiration.
