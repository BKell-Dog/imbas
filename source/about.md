### Artists, Engineers, Builders

{{ site.title }} is an electronics outsourcing and consulting firm that goes deep on complicated problems to deliver elegant solutions. We are a *small team of tech experts* who left the corporate world and now help businesses enhance their existing systems or build new ones from the ground up. Our members specialize in *RF and digital communications* systems like TCP/IP, Bluetooth and Wifi in IoT, ultra-wideband sensors, or cell carrier connectivity.

The word *Imbas* is of [Old Irish](https://lrc.la.utexas.edu/eieol/iriol/0) origin and means roughly *enlightenment* or *inspiration*. The name reflects our beliefs that the best solutions to seemingly-impossible problems are those which enhance scientific principles using some element of artistic or creative inspiration.

## How We Work

We take on a small number of jobs at one time, and go deep on each one.
