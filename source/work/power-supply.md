### Medical Device Power Supply

A very early startup hried us to help build their medical device. They had an engineer working on the delicate medical sensors and microcontroller, we were to handle everything else, specifically the power supply and battery. (Don't ask what the device did, we're not the doctors here). This project had tight restrictions on board size and weight, and wasn't so tight on costs.
