## Our Speciality

We are especially skilled in *connected hardware and wireless systems*, like antenna design, Wifi & Bluetooth devices, or system CAN bus comms. Most teams can get a barebones wireless device up and running easily, but does your device hold up *under stress*? Or is connectivity data painfully slow?
